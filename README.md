
HITFIT is an exercise tracking app developed in SwiftUI, designed to help users manage their workout sessions efficiently. Users can perform exercises with a built-in timer and track their progress over time. The app displays the exercise history in an intuitive graphical format, allowing users to visualize their past workouts. Additionally, HITFIT provides a dedicated graph showing usersâ€™ exercise performance over the last 7 days, helping them stay on top of their fitness goals.
